Annotare Guide

Due to reduced staffing levels between 18 Mar - 8 Apr 2016, curation and brokering of sequencing experiments will take up to 15 working days to complete. If your submission requires urgent processing, please contact us at annotare@ebi.ac.uk and we will raise its priority. Thank you for your understanding and we apologise for any inconvenience caused.

Annotare is a tool for submitting functional genomics experiments to ArrayExpress in MAGE-TAB format.

A series of webforms (auto-saved as you type) will guide you as you describe the experiment's context, sample annotations, protocols employed, and upload raw/processed data files. Use features such as "Fill down values" and "Import Values" to populate multiple rows of form efficiently. A built-in validation step ensures your submission is in a good shape prior to submission, which means we can issue you an accession number and load the data set into ArrayExpress much quicker.
