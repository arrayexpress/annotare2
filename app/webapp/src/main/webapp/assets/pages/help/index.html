Annotare Guide

Annotare is a tool for submitting functional genomics experiments to ArrayExpress in MAGE-TAB format.

A series of webforms (auto-saved as you type) will guide you as you describe the experiment's context, sample annotations, protocols employed, and upload raw/processed data files. Use features such as "Fill down values" and "Import Values" to populate multiple rows of form efficiently. A built-in validation step ensures your submission is in a good shape prior to submission, which means we can issue you an accession number and load the dataset into ArrayExpress much quicker.
